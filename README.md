# Wealth — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026/05/15

Wealth is an iOS app for tracking personal net worth and planning financial independence. This policy describes how the app handles information.

## What stays on your device

All financial data you enter — net worth, goals, FIRE calculator inputs, bookmarks, glossary progress — is stored **only on your device** using iOS local storage (SwiftData and UserDefaults). It is never transmitted to us, synced to a cloud, or shared. We do not run accounts or backend servers.

## Third-party data collection

**Google AdMob** — If you tap "Watch a Short Ad" on the premium paywall, Google AdMob loads a rewarded ad and may collect device identifiers and other data for advertising purposes per [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). The first time this happens, iOS will ask whether to allow tracking; declining serves non-personalized ads.

**Apple StoreKit** — The one-time premium purchase is processed by Apple. We receive a transaction confirmation but no payment information.

## Children's privacy

The app collects no data, so it collects none from children. If you believe a child has used the app, deleting it removes all locally-stored data.
